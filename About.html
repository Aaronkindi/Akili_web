Our story

Akili grew out of an idea by 2 university students in 2024, trying to help small businesses compete in an increasingly fast-paced and digital world.Since then we have helped a handful of small businesses launch world-class websites and grow their brands further.

We are not just an agency — we are a studio of creatives building meaningful digital products for startups, companies, and ourselves.
